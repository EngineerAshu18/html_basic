Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT) in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, is a state university established in 2013. It evolved from the Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, which was founded in 1962. Known for its emphasis on technical education, MMMUT offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, and applied sciences. The university is equipped with modern infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories, a library, hostels, and sports facilities. It is committed to fostering innovation, research, and professional growth among students.
