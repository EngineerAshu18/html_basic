Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), located in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, is a prestigious state technical university established in 2013. It evolved from the renowned Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, which was founded in 1962, and has a legacy of excellence in technical education spanning over six decades. The university offers a wide range of academic programs, including undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA, M.Sc.), and doctoral (Ph.D.) degrees in various fields of engineering, technology, applied sciences, and management. Its curriculum emphasizes practical learning, research, and innovation to prepare students for competitive global industries. MMMUT boasts a sprawling green campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including advanced laboratories, a well-stocked central library, modern classrooms, computer centers, and residential facilities for students and faculty. The university also encourages extracurricular and co-curricular activities through various student clubs, technical societies, and cultural events.
